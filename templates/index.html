Commands

help

aliases: None

info

aliases: invite, uptime, source, source-code, prefix
arguments: None
description: Gives info about the bot: the prefix, link to source code, invite link, uptime

prof

aliases: profile, stats, height, size, cmpg, hpg
arguments: optional(id/tag/mention/nickname)
description: Shows you your stats (height, cm per growth, cm per min, multiplier, daily reward status). You can also do =prof user (replace 'user' with their id/tag/mention/nickname) to see stats for someone else.

grow

aliases: None
arguments: None
description: Main way to gain cm. Has a cooldown of 10 seconds. Growth is depending on your cm per growth which can be increased by buying items from the shop.

shop

aliases: None
arguments: optional(page number)
description: View the items in the shop. Items in the shop increase your cm per growth. Press the arrows at the bottom to change page. You can also do =shop num (replace 'num' with a page number) to go directly to a certain page.

buy

aliases: None
arguments: item
description: Buy an item from the shop. Use in the form: =buy item (replace 'item' with the item you want).

idle-shop

aliases: ishop, i-shop, idle_shop
arguments: optional(page number)
description: View the items in the idle-shop. Items in the idle-shop increase your cm per minute. Press the arrows at the bottom to change page. You can also do =idle-shop num (replace 'num' with a page number) to go directly to a certain page.

idle-buy

aliases: ibuy, idle_buy
arguments: item
description: Buy an item from the idle-shop. Use in the form: =idle-buy item (replace 'item' with the item you want).
